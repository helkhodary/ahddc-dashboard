POC Test Breakdown — Whole Hospital Main: 433 tests · Satellite: 653 tests · Total: 1,086
⚡ AHD runs two distinct POCT programs: the Main hospital (433 POC tests) is driven by Glucose Random, Urine Dipstick, and Blood Gas panels — critical care bedside monitoring in ICU and ED. The Satellite network (653 POC tests) is dominated by Rapid Strep (512) and Influenza A&B (58) — acute ambulatory infectious disease. These are functionally separate programs requiring different devices, workflows, and QC protocols. A single hospital-wide POCT policy does not serve both contexts equally.
